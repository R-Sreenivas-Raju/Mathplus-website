Learning Formats

One-on-One or Group?
Find the right fit for your child.

Highly Personalized

One-on-One Tuition

Best suited for students who require an individualized pace, custom syllabus, or targeted support to master math concepts.

Best For:

  • Students enrolled in an Honours or Advanced programme who need deep enrichment.
  • Students who need targeted help to master the basics and bridge learning gaps.

Key Features:

  • Customizable Syllabus: Tailor the course content at any point (with a 3-day notice) to align with school exams or topics.
  • Flexible Frequency: Choose your learning pace with 1, 2, or 3 classes per week.
  • Same-Day Recording: Receive a recorded link of the class on the same day for review and self-study.
Collaborative & Structured

Group Class (Max 5 Students)

Best suited for students who thrive in a structured, pace-driven environment alongside a small group of peer learners.

Best For:

  • Students planning to learn a Math course ahead of their school year curriculum.
  • Students who enjoy collaborative learning in a structured setting.

Key Features:

  • Fixed Syllabus: A pre-determined path aligned with the US Common Core Standards or AP College Board guidelines.
  • Consistent Pace: Classes are fixed at exactly 2 sessions per week to ensure steady progress.
  • Absence Backup: If a student misses a class, they receive a recording of the live session so they never fall behind.
